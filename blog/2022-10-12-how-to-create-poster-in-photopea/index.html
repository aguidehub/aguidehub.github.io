To create a poster in photopea, when you insert a page and choose the paint bucket tool click on the page and choose the rectangle tool, draw on the page. It will create a poster.

Today, I am going to show you how I create a poster in photopea.

Table of contents

  • New page.
  • Choose the paint bucket tool.
  • Choose the rectangle tool.
  • Insert image.
  • Choose the type-tool.

Let‚Äôs start with the first step.

Step 1: New page.

Go to Photopea.com to start a free online photo editor. And create a new page.

Step 2: Choose the paint bucket tool.

Select the paint bucket tool from the toolbar on the left side of your workspace. then click on the page and change the color.

Step 3: Choose the rectangle tool.

Select the rectangle tool from the toolbar on the left side of your workspace. Click with the mouse and draw some shapes. Then, release the mouse.

Go to the top bar, click on fill then change the color of the rectangle.

Step 4: Insert image.

Open the photopea and Select photo, it will open a file browser or download folder you can select the image. And click the open button.

Step 5: Choose the type-tool.

Select the type-tool from the toolbar on the left side of your workspace. Type your text on the page and change the font style if you want.

Double-click on the layer and it will open the dialogue box set the inner shadow, drop the shadow then press the ok button.

Final result.

When you follow the above steps then it will create a poster in photopea.
