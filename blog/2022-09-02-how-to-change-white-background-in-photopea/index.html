How to change white background in photopea?

September 02, 2022

Hello Friends 👋,

Welcome To aGuidehub! ❤️

To change the white background in photopea, click on the magic wand tool and select the image, then click on the color picker, It will change the white background of the selected area.

Today, I am going to show you how I change white background in photopea.

Table of contents

  • Insert image.
  • Select magic wand tool.
  • Select paint-bucket-tool.

Let’s start with the first step.

Step 1: Insert image.

Open the photopea and Select photo, it will open a file browser or download folder you can select the image. And click open button.

Step 2: Select magic wand tool.

Go to the toolbar and select the magic wand tool and click on your image.

Click the background to select it.

Conversely, lower the Tolerance value if the Magic Wand selected parts of your image you didn’t want to select. Once satisfied with the selection, simply press delete or backspace on your keyboard and watch your background disappear.

Click on the color picker and select the color of your choice then click on the OK button.

Step 3: Select paint-bucket-tool.

Go to the toolbar and select the paint-bucket-tool and click on your image.

Click select in the menu bar and press the deselect. Then your image will be deselected.

Here we have changed the white background color.

When you follow the above steps then it will change white background in photopea.
