Step 1: Created a new page

In Photoshop, go up to the File menu in the Menu Bar, choose new, and then made new page. A window opens that prompts you to name your new document and enter dimensions and preferences.

How to add new page in photoshop?

Step 2: Upload multiple images.

Than Go to File > Open or press CTRL + O,

This will open a file browser or download folder you can use the images. you can choose multiple images, stap by stap.

How to add images in photoshop?

For right side view, than left click mouse on selected layer, And open side bar, click on duplicate layer.

Select another name in the Duplicate layer box. Click the Destination Document icon and select your title page. Click OK.

Step 3: How to make duplicate layer

Go to Layer -> click on Duplicate layer or ctrl+D,

If you create a duplicate layer than go menu bar select you layer, click on layer menu and click on duplicate layer.

Step 4: Select layer in photoshop

You can do this easily by pressing down the CTRL key and selecting all layers.

Now, press ctrl+G,

You can rename the group about your project name.

Finally you have successfully completed your template

When you follow the above steps it will load multiple images as layers and will appear on multiple images layer available on Photoshop right side section.
